In the original ARPANET, ____ were directly connected together.

(A) ** IMPs

(B) Host computers

(C) Networks

(D) Routers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The host computers were connected to the IMPs via custom serial communication interfaces. The system, including the hardware and the packet switching software, was designed and installed in nine months. The BBN team continued to interact with the NPL team with meetings between them taking place in the U.S. and the U.K.

Concept note-2: -The first node of the ARPANET was established when networking hardware was installed to UCLA and connected to a host computer on September 2, 1969, but its birthdate is taken from when the first transmission was made, October 29, 1969.

Concept note-3: -It is the first internet. It was the first wide-area packet-switching network with distributed control and one of the first networks to implement the TCP/IP protocol suite. Hence, Statement 2 is correct.

Concept note-4: -The Advanced Research Projects Agency (ARPA), an arm of the U.S. Defense Department, funded the development of the Advanced Research Projects Agency Network (ARPANET) in the late 1960s. Its initial purpose was to link computers at Pentagon-funded research institutions over telephone lines.
