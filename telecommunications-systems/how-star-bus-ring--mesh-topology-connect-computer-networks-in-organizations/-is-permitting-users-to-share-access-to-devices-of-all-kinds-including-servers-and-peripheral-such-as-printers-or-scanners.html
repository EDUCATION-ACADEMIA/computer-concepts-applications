HOW STAR BUS RING MESH TOPOLOGY CONNECT COMPUTER NETWORKS IN ORGANIZATIONS

[SOURCES]
____ is permitting users to share access to devices of all kinds, including servers and peripheral such as printers or scanners.

(A) Local Area Network

(B) ** Device Sharing

(C) Sharing

(D) Ethernet

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Device sharing refers to a primary purpose for networking: permitting users to share access to devices of all kinds, including servers and peripherals such as printers or plotters.

Concept note-2: -Resource sharing:-This is the main aim of a computer network. It means to make all programs peripherals and data available to any one computer on the network to all other computers in the network without regard to the physical locations of them.

Concept note-3: -A server is a computer program or device that provides a service to another computer program and its user, also known as the client.

Concept note-4: -Wide area network A WAN can extend to any area of the globe. For example, an organization with a corporate office in New York can connect a branch location in London in the same WAN. Users in both locations obtain access to the same data, files and applications, and can communicate with each other.

Concept note-5: -File and Printer Sharing is a Windows operating system feature that enables your computer to communicate to each other and send print jobs to your printer. All computers should be part of the main network and the same Workgroup or Homegroup for File and Printer Sharing to work properly.
