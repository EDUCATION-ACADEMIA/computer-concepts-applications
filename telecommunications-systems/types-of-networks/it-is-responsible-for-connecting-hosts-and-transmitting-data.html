EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Gateway. A gateway is a network node that connects discrete networks or systems that use different protocols, enabling data to flow between the networks.

Concept note-2: -A router is a communications device that connects multiple computers or other routers together and transmits data to its correct desti-nation on the network.

Concept note-3: -There are five kinds of connecting devices: repeaters, hubs, bridges, and two-and three-layer switches. Repeaters and hubs operate in the first layer of the Internet model. Bridges and two-layer switches operate in the first two layers. Routers and three-layer switches operate in the first three layers.

Concept note-4: -The transport layer Layer 4 is responsible for end-to-end communication between the two devices. This includes taking data from the session layer and breaking it up into chunks called segments before sending it to layer 3.
