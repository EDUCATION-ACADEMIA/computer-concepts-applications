NEW DOCUMENTS TEMPLATES IN MICROSOFT WORD

[SOURCES]
To run the spelling checker on your Word document, begin by clicking the Spelling & Grammar button, which is located on the ____ tab.

(A) View

(B) Page Layout

(C) Home

(D) ** Review

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On the Review tab, click Spelling & Grammar. If Word finds a potential error, the Spelling & Grammar dialog box will open, spelling errors will be shown as red text, and grammatical errors will be shown as green text. To fix an error, do one of the following: Type the correction in the box and then click Change.

Concept note-2: -Go to “File.” You can find this towards the top left of your screen in Word’s toolbar. Click on “Options.” This prompts a drop-down menu to appear. Click “Proofing.” After you do this, a small window appears. Check the spell checking option.

Concept note-3: -right-hand side of the main window. The word is automatically entered and the definition, if it can be found, is also shown as well. Adding Comments • Click where in the document you would like to add a comment.

Concept note-4: -Tip: To check the spelling and grammar in just a sentence or paragraph, select the text you want to check and then press F7.
