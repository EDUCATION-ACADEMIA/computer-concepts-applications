Which of the following click tab helps us work with our text, change paragraph styles, and search words?

(A) ** Home Tab

(B) Insert Tab

(C) File Tab

(D) View Tab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Home tab: The Home tab is the default tab in Microsoft Word. It has five groups of related commands; Clipboard, Font, Paragraph, Styles and Editing. It helps you change document settings like font size, adding bullets, adjusting styles and many other common features.

Concept note-2: -Select the text that you want to change. On the Home tab, in the Font group, choose the arrow next to Font Color, and then select a color. You can also use the formatting options on the Mini toolbar to quickly format text.

Concept note-3: -Reset the paragraph settings On the Home tab or on the Page Layout tab, click the Paragraph Dialog Box Launcher in the Paragraph group. On the Indents and Spacing tab, change the Indent settings to the indentation settings that you want for the document.

Concept note-4: -The Home Tab contains the most frequently used commands in Word. To get to another tab on the Ribbon click that particular tab. commands, there is the word “Font”, click the arrow beside it to open a Font Dialog Box, select “Emboss” and click OK. Concept note-5: -On the Home tab, in the Styles group, right-click the style that you want to change, and then click Update [Style Name] to Match Selection. Note: All text with the style that you changed will automatically change to match the new style that you defined.
