An image that is inserted as part of a line of text

(A) ** Inline Graphic

(B) Floating Graphic

(C) Inserted Graphic

(D) Text Graphic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An inline image is a small picture that appears in the flow of the text, for example, an icon that appears inside a paragraph.

Concept note-2: -Inline figures. In line with text, or inline, is the default behavior for pictures, clip art, charts, and SmartArt.

Concept note-3: -Inline images are images that appear inside a block of HTML text. The simplest way to add an inline image in HTML is to insert the img tag into the text exactly where you want the image to appear, as in Figure 30.1.

Concept note-4: -An inline image is an image that is in the body of your email. It is not an attachment. To add an image to your email: Create a new email message, Accept an email message, or Reply to an email message. Click Insert Image .
