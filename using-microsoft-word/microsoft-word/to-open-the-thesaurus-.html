USING MICROSOFT WORD

MICROSOFT WORD

[SOURCES]
To open the Thesaurus ..

(A) ** Shift + F7

(B) F7

(C) Shift + T

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -If you’re on a laptop, you may also need to hold the Fn (Function) key as you press F7 to activate the shortcut. For the thesaurus, just select a word, then press Shift+F7. Watch the video below to see these shortcuts in action. If playback doesn’t begin shortly, try restarting your device.

Concept note-2: -Shift+F7 Runs a Thesaurus check on the selected word. Shift+F12 Save the open document.

Concept note-3: -F7 – Spelling, Grammar etc Shift+F7: Displays the thesaurus pane. If you have selected a word and pressed this function key it will look up the selected word in the Thesaurus pane. Alt+F7: Checks for the next spelling or grammar error in your word document. Alt+Shift+F7: Opens the Translation pane.

Concept note-4: -Word. Click the word in your document that you want to look up. On the Review tab, click Thesaurus.

Concept note-5: -What happens when you press Ctrl F7? In Microsoft programs such as Microsoft Word, Outlook for checking spellings and grammar, the F7 is commonly used. Shift + F7 = Choose the Thesaurus command.
