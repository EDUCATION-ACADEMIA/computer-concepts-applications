What is the answer to the following calculation:5+2*(20-10)/2?

(A) 65

(B) 35

(C) ** 15

(D) 20

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -5*(2+16)/2-4*(2+2)+1/4 of 16 where * means multiplication. Using BODMAS, first of all we will solve bracketts. Hence, the answer for the expression is 33.

Concept note-2: -Expert-Verified Answer Ans :-The value of (20/5) / 2 + (16/8) * 2 + (10/5)(3 + 2) is 16.

Concept note-3: -The BODMAS rule states we should calculate the Brackets first (2 + 4 = 6), then the Orders (52 = 25), then any Division or Multiplication (3 x 6 (the answer to the brackets) = 18), and finally any Addition or Subtraction (18 + 25 = 43).

Concept note-4: -Both BODMAS and PEMDAS are correct and used in different areas of the world. BODMAS is common in the UK while PEMDAS is used in the US. BODMAS stands for Brackets, Order, Division, Multiplication, Addition, Subtraction.
