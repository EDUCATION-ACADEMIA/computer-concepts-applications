What does this symbol do:/

(A) Multiply

(B) Add

(C) ** Divide

(D) Minus

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The symbol for division, or sharing into equal groups, is ÷. It’s called the division symbol.

Concept note-2: -➗ Meaning – Heavy Division Sign Emoji The Heavy Division Sign Emoji was added to the Symbols category in 2010 as part of Unicode 6.0 standard. This is a mature emoji and it should work on most devices.

Concept note-3: -The diagonal slash “/” used as the bar between numerator and denominator of an in-line fraction (Bringhurst 1997, p. 284). The solidus is also called a diagonal.

Concept note-4: -Division. The division slash ⟨ ∕ ⟩, equivalent to the division sign ⟨ ÷ ⟩, may be used between two numbers to indicate division. For example, 23 ÷ 43 can also be written as 23 ∕ 43.

Concept note-5: -Division (/) The division ( / ) operator produces the quotient of its operands where the left operand is the dividend and the right operand is the divisor.
