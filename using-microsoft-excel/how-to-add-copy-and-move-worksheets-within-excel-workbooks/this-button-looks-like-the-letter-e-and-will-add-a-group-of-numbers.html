USING MICROSOFT EXCEL

HOW TO ADD COPY AND MOVE WORKSHEETS WITHIN EXCEL WORKBOOKS

[SOURCES]
This button looks like the letter “E” and will add a group of numbers.

(A) autofill

(B) ** autosum

(C) autocomplete

(D) autodrag

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -AutoSum is in two locations: Home > AutoSum, and Formulas > AutoSum. Once you create a formula, you can copy it to other cells instead of typing it over and over.

Concept note-2: -The symbol “Ʃ” indicates the auto sum in MS Excel. Excel automatically enters a formula to sum the numbers while using the Auto sum. The AutoSum button is available in the ‘Home’ tab on the excel ribbon. It is also available in the ‘Formulas’ tab on the excel ribbon.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is option 1, i.e. The AutoSum button.

Concept note-4: -Excel has a built-in function called Exp() dedicated to calculations involving Euler’s number. Simply put the EXP(x) function returns the value of e raised to the power of ‘x’. Here, ‘x’ can be a simple number like 5 or 9 or even a complex formula like y+5.
