What was the major disadvantage of storing data on paper?

(A) papers could get lost

(B) papers always had errors

(C) it was expensive to store information on paper

(D) ** it was time consuming to sort through

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -One of the primary disadvantages of a traditional paper filing system is the time it takes to access documents. An employee will spend a significant amount of time to retrieve a particular paper record stored in a massive paper filing system.

Concept note-2: -Disadvantages of having a paper-based system Storage space: As paper is by nature physical, it takes up a lot of storage space. With its limited resources, a small business may not be able to afford to rent or purchase storage space onsite or offsite.

Concept note-3: -Security Issues A big obstacle that comes alongside paper records is the security risk. Paper documents are easy to lose, mishandle or damage when in use.
