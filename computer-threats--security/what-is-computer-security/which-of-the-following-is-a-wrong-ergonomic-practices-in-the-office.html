Which of the following is a WRONG ergonomic practices in the office?

(A) The computer monitor should be positioned such as the top no higher than eye level.

(B) ** Tilt your head a bit forward instead of directly above your neck

(C) The keyboard and the mouse should close enough to your body

(D) Make sure that the weight of your arms is supported at all times.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Under the desk, make sure there’s clearance for your knees, thighs and feet. If the desk is too low and can’t be adjusted, place sturdy boards or blocks under the desk legs. If the desk is too high and can’t be adjusted, raise your chair. Use a footrest to support your feet as needed.

Concept note-2: -Frequent lifting. Poor posture. Awkward movements, especially if they are repetitive. Using too much force, especially if it’s done frequently.
