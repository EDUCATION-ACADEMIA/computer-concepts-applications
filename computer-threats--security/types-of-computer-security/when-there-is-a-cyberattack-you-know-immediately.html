TYPES OF COMPUTER SECURITY

[SOURCES]
When there is a cyberattack, you know immediately.

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A cyber attack can maliciously disable computers, steal data, or use a breached computer as a launch point for other attacks. Cybercriminals use a variety of methods to launch a cyber attack, including malware, phishing, ransomware, denial of service, among other methods.

Concept note-2: -Breach Discovery According to IBM, it takes a company 197 days to discover the breach and up to 69 days to contain it. compared to those that took more than 30 days. A slow response to a data breach can cause even more trouble for your company. It can result in a loss of customer trust, productivity, or major fines.

Concept note-3: -The firewalls, antivirus software, and intrusion detection systems (IDS) of these systems help cyber-security experts detect unauthorized transactions within the network [1]. IDSs can be examined under three headings as exploitation detection, anomaly detection, and hybrid systems according to their working methods.

Concept note-4: -The different types of cyber-attacks are malware attack, password attack, phishing attack, and SQL injection attack.
