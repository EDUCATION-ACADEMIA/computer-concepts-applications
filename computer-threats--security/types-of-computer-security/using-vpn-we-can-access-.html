TYPES OF COMPUTER SECURITY

[SOURCES]
Using VPN, we can access ____

(A) ** Access sites that are blocked geographically

(B) Compromise other’s system remotely

(C) Hide our personal data in the cloud

(D) Encrypts our local drive files while transferring

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: With the help of VPN, users can access and connect to sites that are kept blocked by the ISPs based on a specific geographic location.

Concept note-2: -VPN technology, like VyprVPN, allows you to access your favorite sites from anywhere in the world and bypass local blocks and mass Internet censorship. Encrypting your Internet connection not only allows you to secure your personal and private data, but enables you to defeat online censorship and blocks.

Concept note-3: -Explanation: VPNs are not the backbone of the Internet as they are just a method to create private intranets on the internet. They are used for enhancing the connection security for the users.

Concept note-4: -VPNs change your perceived location by swapping out your real IP address with that of the VPN server. IP addresses are unique sequences of numbers and decimals that identify every device on the internet. An IP address can be used to approximate a device’s location.

Concept note-5: -A VPN can hide your online identity by masking your IP address. It encrypts your location and the data you send and receive, helping protect your personal identifiable information (PII).
