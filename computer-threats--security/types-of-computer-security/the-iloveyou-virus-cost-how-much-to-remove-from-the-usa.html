The ILOVEYOU virus cost how much to remove from the USA?

(A) $20 million

(B) ** $15 million

(C) $5

(D) 3 fiddy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Final estimates of the impact of the ILOVEYOU virus are as follows: up to 10% of internet-connected computers were infected, and the total damage, including the destructive actions of its variants, is estimated at about $10 billion.

Concept note-2: -The cost to remove an infection is greatly going to depend on the infection(s) and the damage that has been done. The average removal will cost between $60 and $120 (When the work is done in my office).

Concept note-3: -In just about 10 days, ILOVEYOU reached an estimated 45 million users and caused about $10 billion in damages.

Concept note-4: -The cited ILOVEYOU virus damage was mostly the time and effort paid to handle infection and recover files from backups. In order to protect themselves and stop the ILOVEYOU virus, the British Parliament, The Pentagon, CIA, and most other large companies decided to completely shut down their mail systems.
