What is e-mail?

(A) ** method of exchanging message via electronic devices

(B) speed message transfer to location

(C) musical messging service

(D) none of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Email (electronic mail) is the exchange of computer-stored messages from one user to one or more recipients via the internet. Emails are a fast, inexpensive and accessible way to communicate for business or personal use.

Concept note-2: -Electronic mail (email or e-mail) is a method of exchanging messages ("mail") between people using electronic devices.

Concept note-3: -E-mail is defined as the process of exchanging letters (called as mails) electronically (e). Hence, it is E-mail. You can send the electronic mail to anywhere in the world with in seconds. The person reading it must have a valid internet connection. You can attach images and videos in your e-mail.

Concept note-4: -I. What is Electronic Mail? Electronic mail (e-mail) is a computer-based application for the exchange of messages between users. A worldwide e-mail network allows people to exchange e-mail messages very quickly. E-mail is the electronic equivalent of a letter, but with advantages in timeliness and flexibility.

Concept note-5: -An e-mail system allows computer users on a network to send text, graphics, sounds, and animated images to other users.
