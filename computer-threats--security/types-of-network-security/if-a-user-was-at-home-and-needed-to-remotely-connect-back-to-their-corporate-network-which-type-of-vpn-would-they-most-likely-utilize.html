If a user was at home and needed to remotely connect back to their corporate network, which type of VPN would they most likely utilize?

(A) ** Client to site

(B) Site to client

(C) Site to Site

(D) AES

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It connects devices to networks. For instance, an employee working from home would use a remote access VPN to connect their laptop to their company’s network. This type for VPN can create a more secure connection between remote workers and the organization where they work.

Concept note-2: -A remote access virtual private network (VPN) enables users who are working remotely to securely access and use applications and data that reside in the corporate data center and headquarters, encrypting all traffic the users send and receive.

Concept note-3: -Site-to-site VPN Site-to-site VPNs are mainly used in large companies. They are complex to implement and do not offer the same flexibility as SSL VPNs. However, they are the most effective way to ensure communication within and between large departments.

Concept note-4: -The acronym VPN stands for “Virtual Private Network”. Used often by employees working from home, a VPN connection allows employees to remotely and securely connect to your business’ network. It creates a secure tunnel between their computer and your corporate network.

Concept note-5: -8. type of VPNs are used for home private and secure connectivity. Explanation: Remote access VPN allows individual users to connect to private networks at home and access resources remotely.
