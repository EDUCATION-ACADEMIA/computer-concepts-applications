WHAT IS A COMPUTER VIRUS

[SOURCES]
Our computer can be infected by a virus through:

(A) Wire

(B) Phone

(C) ** CD/DVD

(D) Wifi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Whenever you copy files or download files from an infected removable medium like USB, CD, DVD drives then the virus may enter into your system. Always scan the removable medium before you open.

Concept note-2: -So no, in your example a typical commercial CD/DVD is read-only, so it can’t become infected with malware after the fact even when inserted into a CD/DVD ROM writeable type drive on your PC.

Concept note-3: -A computer virus cannot infect files on write-protected discs like CD-ROMs.

Concept note-4: -Yes it can scan the cd/dvd if you open up My Computer and right click on the cd/dvd drive you’ll see an option to “Scan for Viruses” click on that and SEP will scan the entire disk and flag and files it see’s as a potential risk.
