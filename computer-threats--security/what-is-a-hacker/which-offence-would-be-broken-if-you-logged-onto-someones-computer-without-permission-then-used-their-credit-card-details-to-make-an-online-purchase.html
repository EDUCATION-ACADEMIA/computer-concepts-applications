Which offence would be broken if you logged onto someones computer without permission then used their credit card details to make an online purchase?

(A) Offence 1

(B) ** Offence 2

(C) Offence 3

(D) Offence 4

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The criminal was charged under Section 66C. Section 66D – Cheating by Personation Using Computer Resources. Punishment if found guilty can be imprisonment up to three years and/or up to Rs 1 lakh fine.

Concept note-2: -Unauthorized computer access, popularly referred to as hacking, describes a criminal action whereby someone uses a computer to knowingly gain access to data in a system without permission to access that data. Hacking is illegal under both California and federal law, and can result in heavy penalties.

Concept note-3: -cybercrime, also called computer crime, the use of a computer as an instrument to further illegal ends, such as committing fraud, trafficking in child pornography and intellectual property, stealing identities, or violating privacy.

Concept note-4: -India Code: Section Details. [66. Computer related offences.–If any person, dishonestly or fraudulently, does any act referred to in section 43, he shall be punishable with imprisonment for a term which may extend to three years or with fine which may extend to five lakh rupees or with both.
