Concept note-1: -The GDPR further states that the organization collecting data should ensure it’s accurate and updated as necessary. Concept note-3: -The Data Protection Act 1998 (’the Act’) regulates how and when information relating to individuals may be obtained, used and disclosed. The Act also allows individuals access to personal data relating to them, to challenge misuse of it and to seek redress.
