You can change the ____ to get a wider variety of results on the search engine.

(A) Menu

(B) File

(C) Options

(D) ** Keywords

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Keywords are the words and phrases that people type into search engines to find what they’re looking for. For example, if you were looking to buy a new jacket, you might type something like “mens leather jacket” into Google. Even though that phrase consists of more than one word, it’s still a keyword.

Concept note-2: -Keywords are the terms that people use to search for the information they want to find using search engines like Google or Bing. When somebody searches for a certain term, the search engine looks at the most relevant websites related to that term and returns a list of results.

Concept note-3: -A search engine results page, or SERP, is the page you see after entering a query into Google, Yahoo, or any other search engine. Each search engine’s SERP design is different, but since Google is the most popular-holding over 80% of the market share-we’ll focus on their features and algorithms.
