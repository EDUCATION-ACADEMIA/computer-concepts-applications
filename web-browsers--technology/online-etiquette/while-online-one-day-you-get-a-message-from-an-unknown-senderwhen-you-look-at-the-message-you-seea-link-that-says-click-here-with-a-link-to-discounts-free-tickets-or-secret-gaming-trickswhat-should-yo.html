While online one day, you get a message from an unknown sender.When you look at the message, you seea link that says “Click here” with a link to “discounts, “ “free tickets, “ or “secret gaming tricks”.What should you do?

(A) Click on the link and see what it is.

(B) Forward the email to your friends and win it together!

(C) ** Delete the message and block sender

(D) Reply back to the sender to ask for more info.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -WhatsApp has a feature that helps you prevent the sender from knowing that you have read a message. The feature is simply turning off the read receipt for WhatsApp.

Concept note-2: -The reason why you’re getting messages from unknown numbers on WhatsApp isn’t always straightforward, as there are several reasons why this might be happening. It could be a case of mistaken identity, a spam or phishing message, or even an attempt by hackers or scammers to gain access to your personal information.

Concept note-3: -You only have to turn on the airplane mode of your device or shut down the internet. After that, you can read the message, and it will not show the read receipt to the sender.

Concept note-4: -It is vital to note here that marking a message as ‘unread’ only means that your messages are marked unread only for you. The sender will continue seeing the Blue Ticks indicating that you have read the messages but not replied.
