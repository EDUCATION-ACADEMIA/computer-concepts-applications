Be respectful in how you talk, look, and what you may write.

(A) ** True

(B) False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Be polite, avoid interrupting or causing disturbances. Listen to others and respect differences in beliefs and opinions. Think before you speak, your language and tone. Lend a helping hand or ear and practice compassion.

Concept note-2: -Respect means that you accept somebody for who they are, even when they’re different from you or you don’t agree with them. Respect in your relationships builds feelings of trust, safety, and wellbeing. Respect doesn’t have to come naturally – it is something you learn.
