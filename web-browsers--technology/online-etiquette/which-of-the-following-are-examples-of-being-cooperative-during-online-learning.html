Which of the following are examples of being cooperative during online learning?

(A) ** Helping peers or teacher when needed

(B) Does not listed or is distracted when others are speaking.

(C) ** Listens carefully when others are speaking

(D) Is NOT on camera (laying down/head down) during instruction

(E) Is on his/her phone

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Two common cooperative learning activities are the think-pair-share method and the jigsaw method. Think-pair-share requires students to think about a problem, discuss it with their group members, then share their thoughts with the class.

Concept note-2: -For example, in an online collaborative learning environment, you can scaffold your students’ learning by monitoring your students’ discussion forums to guide off-topic conversations in the right direction, answer clarifying questions, question students on their understanding, and provide feedback on their progress.

Concept note-3: -Regularly participate in school activities. Show your interest in and support of the school by attending PTA meetings, parent-teacher conferences, school plays, fun days, etc. Volunteer to help with field trips, class parties, fund-raising events and other special activities at school.
