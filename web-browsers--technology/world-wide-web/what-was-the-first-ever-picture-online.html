WORLD WIDE WEB

what was the first ever picture online?

(A) all boys band

(B) ** all girls band

(C) Tim Berners-Lee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Exactly 24 years ago – on 18 July, 1992 – the first picture was uploaded on the web. The photo that captured four slickly dressed women was posted by Tim Burners Lee, the inventor of the World Wide Web. The four women belonged to an all-female comedy band called Les Horrible Cernettes – or the Horrible CERN Girls.

Concept note-2: -The first Instagram post was a photo of South Beach Harbor at Pier 38, posted by Mike Krieger at 5:26 p.m. on July 16, 2010. Systrom shared his first post, a picture of a dog and his girlfriend’s foot, a few hours later at 9:24 p.m.

Concept note-3: -Centuries of advances in chemistry and optics, including the invention of the camera obscura, set the stage for the world’s first photograph. In 1826, French scientist Joseph Nicéphore Niépce, took that photograph, titled View from the Window at Le Gras, at his family’s country home.

Concept note-4: -Google Images (previously Google Image Search) is a search engine owned by Google that allows users to search the World Wide Web for images. It was introduced on July 12, 2001, due to a demand for pictures of the green Versace dress of Jennifer Lopez worn in February 2000.
