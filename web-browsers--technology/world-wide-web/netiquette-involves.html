Netiquette involves

(A) ** good manners in email messages

(B) spelling and punctuation on websites

(C) relevance of a website

(D) all answers are correct

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Netiquette (Online Etiquette) is a set of rules that encourages appropriate and courteous online behavior. These rules are important as they promote communication skills, prevent miscommunications, and help you understand what is socially acceptable when working and collaborating online.

Concept note-2: -Proper netiquette guidelines will ensure you do not offend or infringe on the rights of the recipients of your emails. Most individuals consider themselves to fairly law-abiding citizens, but seem to take more chances when working on the Internet because they feel they are untraceable.

Concept note-3: -Netiquette refers to your Internet etiquette, or the way you behave online and on social media. Because the majority of online communication is completely non-verbal, you cannot see or use facial expressions or tone of voice to back what you are typing.
