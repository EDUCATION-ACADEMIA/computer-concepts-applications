What is the name of the algorithm created by the founders of Google, Sergey Brin and Larry Page?

(A) RankWeb

(B) RankPage

(C) ** PageRank

(D) PageGrade

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Larry Page and Sergey Brin developed PageRank at Stanford University in 1996 as part of a research project about a new kind of search engine. An interview with Héctor García-Molina: Stanford Computer Science Professor and Advisor to Sergey provides background into the development of the page-rank algorithm.

Concept note-2: -One key to Google’s success was the PageRank algorithm developed by Google founders Sergey Brin and Larry Page in 1998.

Concept note-3: -PageRank (PR) is an algorithm used by Google Search to rank websites in their search engine results. PageRank was named after Larry Page, one of the founders of Google. PageRank is a way of measuring the importance of website pages.

Concept note-4: -Iterative ranking algorithms have since become an important part of network theory. PageRank was developed in 1998 by Google’s founders Sergey Brin and Larry Page and its impact has been such that it’s easy to forget that the approach was not entirely novel.

Concept note-5: -The PageRank algorithm or Google algorithm was introduced by Lary Page, one of the founders of Google. It was first used to rank web pages in the Google search engine.
