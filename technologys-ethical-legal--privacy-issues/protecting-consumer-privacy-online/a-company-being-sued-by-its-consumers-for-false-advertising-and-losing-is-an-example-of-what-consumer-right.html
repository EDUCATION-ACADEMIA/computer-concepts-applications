A company being sued by its consumers for false advertising and losing is an example of what consumer right?

(A) Right to safety

(B) ** Right to be informed

(C) Right to a healthy environment

(D) Right to satisfaction of needs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Right to Redress This means that consumers have the right to receive a fair settlement of just claims, including compensation for misrepresentation, shoddy goods or unsatisfactory services.

Concept note-2: -Right to be informed as a consumer right. Right to be informed is one of the most important consumer rights. This means that all details regarding the quality, price, quantity, ingredients/contents, side effects, purity and potency must be provided or made available to the customer.

Concept note-3: -Example of violation of consumer’s right to choose is :If you want to buy toothpaste and the shop owner says that she/he can sell the toothpaste only if you buy a toothbrush. If you are not interested in buying the brush your right to choice is denied.

Concept note-4: -According to Article 25(1) UDHR, ‘everyone has the right to a standard of living adequate for the health and well-being of himself and his family’. This provision sets out some of the elements of this right: a) food; b) clothing; c) housing; d) medical care; and e) necessary social services.
