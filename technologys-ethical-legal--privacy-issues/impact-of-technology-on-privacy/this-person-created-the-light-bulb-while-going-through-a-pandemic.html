This person created the light bulb while going through a pandemic

(A) Abe Lincoln

(B) Johnny Jefferson

(C) ** Thomas Edison

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It was the same Thomas Alva Edison we know who invented the light bulb and changed the course of history.

Concept note-2: -Edison did not actually invent the light bulb, of course. People had been making wires incandesce since 1761, and plenty of other inventors had demonstrated and even patented various versions of incandescent lights by 1878, when Edison turned his attention to the problem of illumination.

Concept note-3: -By January 1879, at his laboratory in Menlo Park, New Jersey, Edison had built his first high resistance, incandescent electric light. It worked by passing electricity through a thin platinum filament in the glass vacuum bulb, which delayed the filament from melting.

Concept note-4: -The famous story goes, Edison failed to refine the light bulb (one of the few creations he merely refined but did not invent) so many times it took him 10, 000 attempts to perfect.
