USING PRIVACY SETTINGS IN SOCIAL MEDIA

[SOURCES]
Clickbait is

(A) An image that makes you want to visit a specific place.

(B) A way to share news articles on social media.

(C) A way to buy things online.

(D) ** An image or headline that tries to get you to click on it, usually for advertising purposes.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Clickbait is a sensationalized headline that encourages you to click a link to an article, image, or video. Instead of presenting objective facts, clickbait headlines often appeal to your emotions and curiosity.

Concept note-2: -Clickbait typically refers to the practice of writing sensationalized or misleading headlines in order to attract clicks on a piece of content. It often relies on exaggerating claims or leaving out key information in order to encourage traffic. The term is generally used in a dismissive sense. Marketing Dictionary. c.

Concept note-3: -Clickbait is primarily used to drive page views on websites, whether for their own purposes or to increase online advertising revenue. It can also be used for phishing attacks for the purpose of spreading malicious files or stealing user information.

Concept note-4: -Clickbait advertising is a form of “bait and switch” advertising designed to lure users into clicking on a link and read, view, or listen to content by providing an attractive but misleading headline. These false headlines often result in the spread of outrageous or inaccurate information on social media.

Concept note-5: -Clickbait is web content with a misleading or sensationalist headline designed to get readers to click through to the full story, which is generally a disappointment. Clickbait’s goal is usually to generate pageviews and advertising revenue.
