What should you ASK about privacy policies?

(A) ** What will be Accessed? What will be Shared with other people/companies? What could be Known about me if I use this?

(B) What are people Asking? What is being Stored about me? Is it Kind?

(C) Is this an Advertisement? Is this a privacy Setting? Is this information Collected?

(D) I don’t need to ask anything about privacy policies; I just click accept.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Your Privacy Policy is where you disclose, at minimum, what personal information you collect from your users, how you collect the information, how you use it, and whether you share it with any third parties. Check almost any website footer and you’ll surely find a link to one of these required agreements.

Concept note-2: -This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can update, manage, export, and delete your information. We build a range of services that help millions of people daily to explore and interact with the world in new ways.

Concept note-3: -In case of any queries regarding the content, interpretation, implications of this Policy/BCR’s, the Relevant Individuals may contact the Grievance Officer.

Concept note-4: -Footer. The footer is the most popular place for websites to put their privacy policy. The footer is also available from any page on your website, which is important when you are complying with international privacy laws.
