When you post a photo, or a video, or a text, how long will people be able to see it?

(A) one month

(B) one week

(C) ** forever

(D) However long you want. You have control.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -However, it is important to remember that anything you post online will basically exist forever. Once something is out on the internet, it can be nearly impossible to delete it completely or to take it back.

Concept note-2: -Photos and videos you share to your story disappear from Feed, your profile and Direct after 24 hours, unless you add it as a highlight. Keep in mind that you can also save photos and videos from your story or share your story to Feed. Was this helpful? How could it be better?

Concept note-3: -It’s called “digital permanence, ” the idea that once something is uploaded, posted, published or otherwise “put on” the Internet, it will be there forever.

Concept note-4: -Each photo or video you share to your story is available in the Stories section on Facebook and the Messenger app for 24 hours. After 24 hours, you can keep the stories you’ve shared in your story archive, which will only be visible to you.
