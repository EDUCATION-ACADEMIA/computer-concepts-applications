Today is a digital age, a age of information and ____

(A) Digital technology

(B) Information age

(C) ** High technology

(D) Privacy unintentionally

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Information Age (also known as the Computer Age, Digital Age, Silicon Age, or New Media Age) is a historical period that began in the mid-20th century. It is characterized by a rapid shift from traditional industries, as established during the Industrial Revolution, to an economy centered on information technology.

Concept note-2: -The industrial revolution brought us to the industrial age, which eventually led to the digital revolution and the current information age we are now in.

Concept note-3: -The Information Age began around the 1970s and is still going on today. It is also known as the Computer Age, Digital Age, or New Media Age. This era brought about a time period in which people could access information and knowledge easily.

Concept note-4: -The 21st Century spans 100 years. Currently, it encompasses the Information Age-an era marked by rapid adoption of new technologies. This Information Age is being fueled by a Knowledge Economy that values problem solving and critical thinking over the rote skills of the Industrial era.
