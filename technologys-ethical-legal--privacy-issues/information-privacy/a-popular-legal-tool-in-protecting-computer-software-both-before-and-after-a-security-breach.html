A popular legal tool in protecting computer software, both before and after a security breach.

(B) Trade secrets

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Trade Secrets: This law protects the private aspects of ideas which is known only to the discover or his confidants. Once disclosed, trade secret is lost as such and is only protected by the law for trade secrets.

Concept note-2: -The definition of online privacy is the level of privacy protection an individual has while connected to the Internet. It covers the amount of online security available for personal and financial data, communications, and preferences.

Concept note-3: -Which one of the following describes how confidential information should be sent using an unsecured network? In an unsigned email.

Concept note-4: -Virus: Viruses are self-replicating programs that attach themselves to clean files, much like a human virus.
