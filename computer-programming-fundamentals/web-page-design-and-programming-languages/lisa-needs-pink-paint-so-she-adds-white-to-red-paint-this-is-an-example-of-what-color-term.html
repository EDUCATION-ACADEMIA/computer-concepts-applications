Lisa needs pink paint so she adds white to red paint. This is an example of what color term?

(A) ** tint

(B) shade

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Analogous colors is another term for complementary colors. Red and green are examples of complementary colors. The color plan of red, yellow and blue is an example of a triad color scheme. Unity is obtained by repeating colors and shapes–all parts of a design are working together as a team.

Concept note-2: -A line is defined as a mark with length and direction, created by a point that moves across a surface. A line can vary in length, width, direction, curvature, and color. Line can be two-dimensional (a pencil line on paper), or implied three-dimensional.

Concept note-3: -What kind of line can push an image forward or backward for the viewer? A horizon or placid feeling. How is a contour line different from an outline? Jacques Callot’s gestural drawing of a horse uses contour lines to do what?

Concept note-4: -Intensity is an art element that means darkness or lightness of a surface. Obtained by adding black to a hue. May be real or simulated as in rubbings or drawn wood-grain. The pure hue is at it’s brightest right from the bottle.
