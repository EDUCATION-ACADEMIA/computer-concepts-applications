To which of the following chart types can you not add axis titles?

(A) ** Pie chart

(B) Line chart

(C) Area chart

(D) Column chart

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You cannot add axis titles to charts that do not have axes (Pie or Doughnut charts).

Concept note-2: -Answer: Fancy is not a chart type.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Bar charts, line graphs, and pie charts. A chart is a powerful tool that allows you to visually display data in a variety of different chart formats. MS Excel can produce different chart formats such as Bar, Pie, Line, Column, Area, Scatter, Surface, or Radar charts.

Concept note-4: -Bar and column charts are used to compare different items. Bars on a column chart are vertical while bars on a bar chart are horizontal.

Concept note-5: -Add titles and data labels to a chart To help clarify the information that appears in your chart, you can add a chart title, axis titles, and data labels. Add a legend or data table You can show or hide a legend, change its location, or modify the legend entries.
