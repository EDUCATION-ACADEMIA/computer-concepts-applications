Which key do you press to move down to the next row on a spreadsheet?

(A) Tab

(B) Backspace

(C) ** Enter

(D) Delete

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To move to the next selected cell, press TAB or ENTER. To move to the previous selected cell, press SHIFT + TAB or SHIFT + ENTER.

Concept note-2: -Use the Enter Key One way to jump to the next row in Excel is to simply press the Enter key on your keyboard after you have finished entering data in the current row. This will automatically take you to the next row where you can begin entering data.

Concept note-3: -Click the location inside the cell where you want to break the line or insert a new line and press Alt+Enter.

Concept note-4: -Press PAGE UP or PAGE DOWN. Press SCROLL LOCK, and then hold down CTRL while you press the LEFT ARROW or RIGHT ARROW key. Press SCROLL LOCK, and then simultaneously hold down CTRL and an arrow key to quickly move through large areas of your worksheet.
