To add rows in Excel, the command clicked on the Insert menu on Home is ____

(A) Insert Cells

(B) ** Insert Sheet Rows

(C) Insert Sheet Column

(D) Insert Sheet

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To insert a new worksheet in front of an existing worksheet, select that worksheet and then, on the Home tab, in the Cells group, click Insert, and then click Insert Sheet. Tip: You can also right-click the tab of an existing worksheet, and then click Insert. On the General tab, click Worksheet, and then click OK.

Concept note-2: -Explanation: Choose Homeâ†’Cells and click the arrow to the right of the Insert button to open the drop-down list for the Insert button. From the menu, choose Insert Sheet Rows or Insert Sheet Columns.

Concept note-3: -Under Table Tools, on the Layout tab, do one of the following: To add a row above the cell, click Insert Above in the Rows and Columns group. To add a row below the cell, click Insert Below in the Rows and Columns group.
