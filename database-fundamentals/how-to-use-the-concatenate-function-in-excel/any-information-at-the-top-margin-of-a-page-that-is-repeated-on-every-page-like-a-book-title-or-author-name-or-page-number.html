HOW TO USE THE CONCATENATE FUNCTION IN EXCEL

[SOURCES]
Any information at the top margin of a page that is repeated on every page like a book title or author name or page number.

(A) Ribbon

(B) COUNTIF

(C) ** Header

(D) Autofill

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Headers and footers generally contain document information, such as the file name, author, date created, page numbering and the like. This information is repeated on each page and thus enables the reader to easily navigate the document.

Concept note-2: -The header is a section of the document that appears in the top margin, while the footer is a section of the document that appears in the bottom margin.

Concept note-3: -Header. Explanation: Text inserted in the top margin of each page of a document is called a header, and text inserted in the bottom margin is called a footer.

Concept note-4: -The text that appears in the topmargin of every page of a document is called. Heading.

Concept note-5: -A header is the top margin of each page, and a footer is the bottom margin of each page. Headers and footers are useful for including material that you want to appear on every page of a document such as your name, the title of the document, or page numbers.
