HOW TO USE THE CONCATENATE FUNCTION IN EXCEL

[SOURCES]
The calculation formula of the following expression 5 4 + 225 1000 is:

(A) =POWER(5;4)+225-1000

(B) =5*4+225-1000

(C) ** =POWER(5;4)+SQRT(225)-1000

(D) IT CANNOT BE CALCULATED IN EXCEL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The square root value of 225 is 15, i.e. √225 = 15. Here, √225 is the radical representation of the square root of 225. We know that the square of 15 is 225, i.e. 152 = 15 × 15 = 225. Thus, the square root of 225 can also be described as, √225 = √(15)2 = √(15 × 15) = 15.

Concept note-2: -Therefore, √225 is a rational number.

Concept note-3: -We write √225=15. If we want to find the negative square root of a number, we place a negative in front of the radical sign.

Concept note-4: -√225 = 15 shown by division method.
