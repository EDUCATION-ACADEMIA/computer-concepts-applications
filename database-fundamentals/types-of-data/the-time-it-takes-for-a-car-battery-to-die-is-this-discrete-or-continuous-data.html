The time it takes for a car battery to die. Is this Discrete or Continuous Data?

(A) Discrete

(B) ** Continuous

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So useful life of a car Battery B is ex continuous or discreet, so that would be continuous months. Time in general is continuous.

Concept note-2: -Examples of continuous data include weight, height, length, time, and temperature.

Concept note-3: -Continuous data is data that can take any value. Height, weight, temperature and length are all examples of continuous data. Some continuous data will change over time; the weight of a baby in its first year or the temperature in a room throughout the day.

Concept note-4: -Between any two points in time there are an infinite number of other points in time. The variable “time” ranges over the entire real number line, or depending on the context, over some subset of it such as the non-negative reals. Thus time is viewed as a continuous variable.
