How many pets do you have?

(A) Categorical

(B) ** Numerical

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Household pet ownership statistics There’s about one cat or dog for every 2.4 people. Approximately 48 million American households own a dog.

Concept note-2: -There is no set number of pets that works for everyone. It all comes down to how much space you have, your ability to pay for their food and vet care, and your ability to care for them. If you can not truly provide for them, you have too many.

Concept note-3: -You are financially unable to meet their needs. This is one of the most apparent signs that you have too many pets to handle. Caring for your pets’ needs means being able to cover the cost of food, supplies, toys and treats, and veterinary care.
