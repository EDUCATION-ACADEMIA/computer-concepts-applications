I score 86 out of 120 in a test. What percentage did I score (to the nearest whole percentage)?

(A) 70

(B) 71

(C) ** 72

(D) 73

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The total answers count 120-it’s 100%, so we to get a 1% value, divide 120 by 100 to get 1.20. Next, calculate the percentage of 86: divide 86 by 1% value (1.20), and you get 71.67%-it’s your percentage grade.

Concept note-2: -71.67% C-A score of 86 out of 120 on a test, assignment or class is a 71.67% percentage grade. 34 questions were wrong or points missed. A 71% is a C-letter grade.

Concept note-3: -Answer and Explanation: 72 is 60 percent of 120.

Concept note-4: -A score of 72 out of 86 on a test, assignment or class is a 83.72% percentage grade. 14 questions were wrong or points missed. A 83% is a B letter grade. A letter grade B means good or above average performance.
