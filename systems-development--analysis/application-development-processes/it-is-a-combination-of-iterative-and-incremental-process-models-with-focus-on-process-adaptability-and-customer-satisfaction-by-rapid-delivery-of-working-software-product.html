It is a combination of iterative and incremental process models with focus on process adaptability and customer satisfaction by rapid delivery of working software product.

(A) ** Agile

(B) Big Bang Model

(C) Iterative Model

(D) Waterfall Model

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Agile Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) is the combination of both iterative and incremental process models. It focuses on process adaptability and customer satisfaction by rapid delivery of working software product. Agile SDLC breaks down the product into small incremental builds.

Concept note-2: -Agile method and methodology is a particular approach to project management that is utilized in software development. It is a combination of iterative and incremental work sequences with a focus on process adaptability and customer satisfaction through the rapid delivery of the working product.

Concept note-3: -Scrum and agile are both incremental and iterative. They are iterative in that they plan for the work of one iteration to be improved upon in subsequent iterations. They are incremental because completed work is delivered throughout the project.
