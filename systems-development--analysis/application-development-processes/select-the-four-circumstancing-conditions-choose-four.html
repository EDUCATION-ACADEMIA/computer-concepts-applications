EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Circumstancing establishes a baseline for expected case behavior and adds variants to address exceptions to the behavior. The goal of circumstancing is to create a variant for each anticipated situation. Pega selects the appropriate variant, or circumstance, to use based on the details of the case.

Concept note-2: -Circumstance means creating multiple variants of a single rule based on condition. Circumstancing requires condition to be applied on the rule. Save. Done.

Concept note-3: -Rule Resolution is the process Pega uses to determine the most appropriate rule to execute. When a rule is referenced in a Pega application, rule resolution attempts to locate instances of the rule in the rules cache.
