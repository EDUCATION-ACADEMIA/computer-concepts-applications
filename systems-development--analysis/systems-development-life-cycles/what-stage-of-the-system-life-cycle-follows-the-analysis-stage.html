(B) Testing

Concept note-1: -There are usually six stages in this cycle: requirement analysis, design, development and testing, implementation, documentation, and evaluation.

Concept note-3: -The systems development life cycle (SDLC) is a conceptual model used in project management that describes the stages involved in an information system development project, from an initial feasibility study through maintenance of the completed application.
