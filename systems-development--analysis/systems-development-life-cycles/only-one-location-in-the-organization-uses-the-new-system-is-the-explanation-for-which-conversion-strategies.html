Only one location in the organization uses the new system‚Äù is the explanation for which conversion strategies?

(A) Parallel

(B) ** Pilot

(C) Phased

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pilot conversion-Only one location in the organization uses the new system-so that it can be tested. After the pilot site approves the new system, other sites convert using one of the other conversion strategies.

Concept note-2: -Direct Conversion: Direct conversion is the implementation of the new system and the immediate discontinuance of the old system.

Concept note-3: -In the phased approach conversion strategy, the new system: replaces the old one at an appointed time. is introduced to a limited area until it is proven to work properly.

Concept note-4: -Conversion is the process of changing from the old system to the new system. Four main conversion strategies can be employed: the parallel strategy, the direct cutover strategy, the pilot study strategy, and the phased approach strategy.
