Burning an image into a consumer’s mind is called

(A) Torture

(B) ** Branding

(C) Advertising

(D) market segmentation

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Branding is the process of creating distinctive and long lasting perceptions in the minds of consumers. A brand is a persistent, unique business identity mixed up with the personality of the consumers, their interest, quality of the product and its origin. It attempts to make people loyal to the product of the firm.

Concept note-2: -Branding is the process of creating a distinct identity for a business in the mind of your target audience and consumers. At the the most basic level, branding is made up of a company’s logo, visual design, mission, and tone of voice.

Concept note-3: -Consumer brand marketing, or consumer branding, is a strategic approach that brands can use to establish familiarity, trust, and added-value for their customers through brand positioning.

Concept note-4: -Brand image is a consumer’s interpretation of your company and its products and services. It takes form inside the consumer’s mind based on their experiences and interactions, as well as their perception of your company’s mission and values.

Concept note-5: -The term derives from the Old Norse word brandr or “to burn, ” and refers to the practice of branding livestock, which dates back more than 4, 000 years to the Indus Valley.
