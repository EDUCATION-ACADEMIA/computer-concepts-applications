If the body of the text is justified, which design principle is being demonstrated?

(A) balance

(B) ** alignment

(C) white space

(D) contrast

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Alignment is one of the most important design principles. It helps ensure a sharp, ordered appearance for ultimately better designs by ensuring your various elements of design have a pleasing connection with each other.

Concept note-2: -Principles of design include emphasis, alignment and balance, contrast, repetition, proportion, movement, and white space.

Concept note-3: -Definition: The principle of proximity states that items close together are likely to be perceived as part of the same group-sharing similar functionality or traits.

Concept note-4: -What is the unity principle of design? Unity is the principle of design that unifies all other principles within a piece of work, allowing each individual element to coexist with one another to form an aesthetically pleasing design.
