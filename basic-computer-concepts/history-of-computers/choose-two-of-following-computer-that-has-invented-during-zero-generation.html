Choose TWO of following computer that has invented during zero generation

(A) IAS

(B) ** Analytical Machine

(C) ** Different Machine

(D) ENIAC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Generation 0: Mechanical Computers (1642-1945) The first working calculating machine was invented in 1623 by German inventor Wilhelm Schickard (1592-1635), although details of its design were lost in a fire soon after its construction.

Concept note-2: -A second-generation computer, through the late 1950s and 1960s featured circuit boards filled with individual transistors and magnetic core memory. These machines remained the mainstream design into the late 1960s, when integrated circuits started appearing and led to the third-generation computer.
