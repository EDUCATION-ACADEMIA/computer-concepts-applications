This is the first portable computer.

(A) ENIAC

(B) UNIVAC 1

(C) ** Osborne 1

(D) Z1

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Osborne 1 is the first commercially successful portable computer, released on April 3, 1981 by Osborne Computer Corporation . It weighs 24.5 lb (11.1 kg), cost US$1, 795, and runs the CP/M 2.2 operating system.

Concept note-2: -Osborne 1 introduced Weighing 24 pounds and costing $1, 795, the Osborne 1 is the first mass-produced portable computer.

Concept note-3: -What is the use of Osborne 1? Osborne 1 was originally designed for portability. Apart from addressing the portability of computers, it also came prepackaged with multiple productivity software applications, such as spreadsheets, word processing, etc.
