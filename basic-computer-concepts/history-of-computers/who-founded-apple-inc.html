Who founded Apple Inc.?

(A) ** Steve Jobs & Steve Wozniak

(B) Bill Gates & Steve Jobs

(C) Steve Wozniak & Bill Gates

(D) Steve Jobs & Douglas Englebart

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Apple Computers, Inc. was founded on April 1, 1976, by college dropouts Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak, who brought to the new company a vision of changing the way people viewed computers.

Concept note-2: -Key Takeaways. Steve Jobs and Steve Wozniak co-founded Apple in 1977, introducing first the Apple I and then the Apple II. Apple went public in 1980 with Jobs the blazing visionary and Wozniak the shy genius executing his vision.

Concept note-3: -The inventor was a guy named Edwin Land and Steve Jobs idolized him and his company and modeled his career after him.

Concept note-4: -While at the helm of Apple, Jobs led the company in developing groundbreaking products, including the iPod, iPhone, and iPad. Jobs founded the NeXT computer company, which developed an operating system that Apple eventually acquired to boost its personal computers.
