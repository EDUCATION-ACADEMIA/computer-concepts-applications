How have computers changed the economy?

(A) ** Computers can store, process and access large amounts of data.

(B) ** The internet allows global communications

(C) ** Smartphones and tablets mean accessing information is even easier.

(D) ** The internet allows people to work from home.

(E) ** 1.3 million people in Britain work in IT

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Industry trends The IT-BPM (Information technology and Business Process Management) sector of India has a share of 9.3% of the country’s GDP, making it one of the biggest sectors to contribute to India’s economic growth. The IT sector captures 56% of the global outsourcing market.

Concept note-2: -In economics, it is widely accepted that technology is the key driver of economic growth of countries, regions and cities. Technological progress allows for the more efficient production of more and better goods and services, which is what prosperity depends on.

Concept note-3: -Information technology connects individuals, firms, and governments via information networks and shared databanks, thereby facilitating the production, distribution, and consumption of information within the whole economy and society.

Concept note-4: -Positive Impact of Computer We can store such a large amount of information on a computer which makes it easy to handle the data for business applications. We can perform multitasking and multiprocessing capabilities of knowledge. It is extremely easy to access and use data for business applications.
