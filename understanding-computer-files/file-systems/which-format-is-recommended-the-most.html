Which format is recommended the most?

(A) Quick format

(B) Full-Quick format

(C) New format

(D) ** Full format

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -RAW files are the highest quality image format. They are loved by photographers as RAW format records all data from the sensor of the camera. Since RAW is an uncompressed format, it gives immense creative liberty to the photographers during post-processing.

Concept note-2: -1. MP4. MP4 is one of the most popular video file formats because of its universal compatibility. It is also capable of playing high-quality videos with a small file size.

Concept note-3: -Developed by Microsoft, AVI offers the highest quality but also large file sizes. It is supported by YouTube and works well for TV viewing. Advanced Video Coding High Definition is specifically for high-definition video.
