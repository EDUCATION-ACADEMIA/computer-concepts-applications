Concept note-1: -Try changing your stationary phase or solvent. If peak tailing is occurring only in peaks closest to the solvent front or major component peak, consider the Reverse Solvent Effect. Try a retention gap or guard column to correct this.

Concept note-2: -Re: No peaks on chromatogram-already checked common causes-h It is possible that the extra flow through the megabore column is upsetting the flame. You may need to adjust fuel or make up flows to compensate. There may also be problems with the fatter column blocking the flame tip.

Concept note-3: -Minimize dead volume in the GC system; verify proper column installation, proper connectors, proper liners, etc. • Verify injector and detector flow rates and adjust if needed. • Verify make-up gas flow and adjust if needed.
