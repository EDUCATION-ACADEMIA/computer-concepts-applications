What is the most important security measure you can take to protect your internet connection from unauthorized users:(KLO 3.2, unit 28)

(A) ** Encrypt password to protect your wireless home network

(B) never share you ID and password

(C) Install antivirus

(D) update OS

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A virtual private network (VPN) is one of the best ways to secure your internet connection. It acts as a middleman between your connected device and the internet. It hides your activity so that no one (even online advertisers) can see what you’re doing online.

Concept note-2: -Use a firewall to protect the devices on your network Most routers come with network firewalls enabled, so check that yours is enabled. If your router doesn’t have a firewall, you can install a good home firewall solution on your system to prevent router attacks.

Concept note-3: -You should be using WPA2 security to guard access to your router, which essentially requires every new device to submit a password to connect. This is enabled by default on just about every router, but if it’s not active on your device, switch it on through your router settings.
