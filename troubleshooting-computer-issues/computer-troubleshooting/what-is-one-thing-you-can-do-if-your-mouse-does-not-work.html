COMPUTER TROUBLESHOOTING

[SOURCES]
What is one thing you can do if your mouse does not work?

(A) buy a wireless mouse

(B) ** check the connections

(C) cut the cord

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Wireless mouse Move your mouse around on the mousepad to see if any indicator comes on. If your mouse has a power switch, it’s often on the bottom side. If the mouse doesn’t appear to be on, replace the batteries. Make sure the wireless receiver is within range and is not blocked by too many objects.

Concept note-2: -Your mouse may be connecting to another device nearby. Your mouse no longer remembers the pairing. There is a problem with your computer/devices Bluetooth adapter. Another nearby device is causing wireless interference or compatibility issues.
