TROUBLESHOOTING COMPUTER ISSUES

COMPUTER TROUBLESHOOTING

[SOURCES]
The power goes out in your building. When everything works again, your devices are connected, but cannot “see” the internet. What should you try first?

(A) ** Confirm internet with ISP using direct ethernet

(B) Power off all devices and power on

(C) Replace all switches immeeditely

(D) Check wifi passwords

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reboot the Computer The first step should be rebooting your computer or device to see if that fixes the problem. You’d be surprised how often that simple step resolves issues.

Concept note-2: -Network troubleshooting is the systematic process of searching for, diagnosing, and correcting network issues.
