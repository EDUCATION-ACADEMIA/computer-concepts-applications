COMPUTER TROUBLESHOOTING

[SOURCES]
What is NOT a solution to fix this computer problem if you monitor has a black screen?

(A) Press the Power button on the monitor

(B) ** Wait for the monitor to turn blue

(C) Move the mouse to reactive the monitor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Usually, the blue screen error is not fatal to your machine, but might turn out to be annoying if you get interrupted while performing some task. The black screen error, also known as the BSOD (Black Screen Of Death), happens mostly because of a faulty device driver, a hardware defect, or a Windows software error.

Concept note-2: -A blue screen error (also called a stop error) can occur if a problem causes your device to shut down or restart unexpectedly. You might see a blue screen with a message that your device ran into a problem and needs to restart.
