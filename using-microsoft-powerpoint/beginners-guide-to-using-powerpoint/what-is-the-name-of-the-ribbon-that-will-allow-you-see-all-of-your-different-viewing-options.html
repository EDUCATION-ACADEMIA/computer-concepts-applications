What is the name of the ribbon that will allow you see all of your different viewing options?

(A) Transitions

(B) Design

(C) ** View

(D) Insert

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is it called when the Ribbon stays visible? Pinned. Which tab is the default active tab? The Home tab.

Concept note-2: -Clicking the icon will allow you to choose from three options: hide the Ribbon completely, show only the ribbon tabs, or show the tabs and commands. If Auto-hide Ribbon is selected, the ribbon will be completely hidden. Clicking the three dots in the upper right corner of your document will restore the Ribbon view.

Concept note-3: -Click the Ribbon Display Options button in the lower-right corner of the ribbon. In the menu that opens, click Show Tabs and Commands to show the Ribbon with all tabs and full commands.

Concept note-4: -The ribbon is a command bar that organizes a programâ€™s features into a series of tabs at the top of the screen. Ribbon tabs are composed of groups of closely related commands, designed to help users quickly find desired commands. Each ribbon is a bar (line) across the page.
