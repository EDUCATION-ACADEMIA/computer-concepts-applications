BEGINNERS GUIDE TO USING POWERPOINT

[SOURCES]
To add a design to your PowerPoint, you should look under the ____ tab.

(A) ** Design

(B) Home

(C) Insert

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ask for design ideas any time by choosing Design > Designer on the ribbon. PowerPoint shows design ideas for your slide. Scroll through the suggestions in the Designer pane on the right side of the window. Click to select the design you want, or else close the window.

Concept note-2: -Apply a design template Open your presentation. Click the Design tab on the ribbon. Click the design you want-the design is applied to the presentation.

Concept note-3: -Customize your theme To find a theme to use in your presentation, click the Design tab, click a theme, and see how it previews on the slide. You can further customize the theme by changing the fonts, colors, and background colors on the Design tab.
