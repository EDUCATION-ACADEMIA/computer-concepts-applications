What is the main advantage of using templates and other presentations to create a new presentation?

(A) They give you access to additional slide layouts that you cannot create in a blank presentation.

(B) They automatically fill-in content and allow you to spend more time on designing slides.

(C) They allow you to include more slides in a presentation.

(D) ** They save you time designing slides and allow you to focus more on the content.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is the main advantage of using templates and other presentations to create a new presentation? They save you time designing slides and allow you to focus more on the content.

Concept note-2: -What Is The Main Advantage Of Using Templates and Other Presentations To Create A New Presentation ? They Save You Time Designing and Allow You To Focus More On The Content . They Give You Access To Additional Slide Layouts That You Cannot Create In A Blank Presentation .

Concept note-3: -A PowerPoint template, or Master, enables the user to maintain consistency of key elements throughout the slideshow. Elements like color scheme, title, text, charts, logos, and images will appear in consistent sizes and designated positions throughout the presentation.

Concept note-4: -Branding: Templates can easily be modified, and that means that branding is simpler than ever. Change colors, fonts, graphics, or even add your own logo to the mix. Concept note-5: -A template is a theme plus some content for a specific purpose-such as a sales presentation, a business plan, or a classroom lesson. So a template has design elements that work together (colors, fonts, backgrounds, effects) along with sample slides and boilerplate content that you augment to tell your story.
