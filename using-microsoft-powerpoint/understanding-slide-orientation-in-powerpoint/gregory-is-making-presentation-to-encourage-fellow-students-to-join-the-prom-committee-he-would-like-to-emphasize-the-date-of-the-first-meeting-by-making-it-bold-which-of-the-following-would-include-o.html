Concept note-1: -PREP can be used for both short and long speeches. Another way to frame PREP is to take each key point and 1) State it, 2) Explain it, 3) Show it, and 4) Conclude it. Those four steps are essentially what you do with PREP. Point (States it), Reason (Explains it), Example (Shows it), and Point (Concludes it).

Concept note-2: -Which of the following techniques will help you make your presentation about people? face the audience directly with a slight forward lean and upright posture during a presentation.

Concept note-3: -A picture looks good in your slide show but pixelated when you print it. How can you fix this? Compress the picture and use the Print (220 ppi) resolution setting.
