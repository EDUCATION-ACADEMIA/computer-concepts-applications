USING MICROSOFT POWERPOINT

USING THE TRANSITIONS AND ANIMATIONS MENUS IN POWERPOINT

[SOURCES]
____ allow you to jump to specific points in the video.

(A) Fade-ins

(B) Fade-outs

(C) ** Bookmarks

(D) Favorites

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bookmarks allow you to save a Video Event to your Timeline indefinitely, and won’t take up any storage space on your smartphone. You can view bookmarked Video Events directly on your Timeline, but you can also filter your Timeline to only view Video Events that you’ve bookmarked. Bookmark a Video.

Concept note-2: -How to Bookmark a Video on YouTube. Bookmarking a video to access it later is simple. In a desktop browser, open the video that you want to save so that your browser has its page URL open. To bookmark the current page in most browsers, including Chrome, you can press Ctrl + D (Cmd + D on a Mac).

Concept note-3: -While you’re delivering a presentation, a bookmark in a media file lets you easily start from that point and play the media for your audience. Here is a bookmark that shows where to trim the beginning of an audio clip: 365, 20212010–2019.
