USING THE TRANSITIONS AND ANIMATIONS MENUS IN POWERPOINT

[SOURCES]
Which key or keys do you press to indent a first-level item in a list so it becomes a second-level item?

(A) shift

(B) shift + tab

(C) spacebar

(D) ** tab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indenting helps to format the layout of text so it appears more organized on your slide. The fastest way to indent is to use the Tab key.

Concept note-2: -A quick way to indent is to use the Tab key. This will create a first-line indent of 1/2 inch. Place the insertion point at the very beginning of the paragraph you want to indent. Press the Tab key.

Concept note-3: -When the insertion point is at the beginning of a new line in a content placeholder, you can press SHIFT+TAB to indent the item.

Concept note-4: -Indent paragraph with shortcut key A complete paragraph can be indented with the keyboard shortcut Ctrl + M and pressing the keyboard shortcut more than once will indent the paragraph further. To reverse the indent, you can also press Ctrl + Shift + M .

Concept note-5: -Right-click, and then click Adjust List Indents. Change the distance of the bullet indent from the margin by clicking the arrows in the Bullet position box, or change the distance between the bullet and the text by clicking the arrows in the Text indent box.
