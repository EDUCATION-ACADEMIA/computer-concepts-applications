USING MICROSOFT POWERPOINT

USING THE TRANSITIONS AND ANIMATIONS MENUS IN POWERPOINT

[SOURCES]
SmartArt can be added by clicking on the SmartArt icon in the ____ group.

(A) Drawing

(B) Editing

(C) ** Illustrations

(D) Slides

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On the Insert tab, in the Illustrations group, click SmartArt. In the Choose a SmartArt Graphic dialog box, click the type and layout that you want. Enter your text by doing one of the following: Click [Text] in the Text pane, and then type your text.

Concept note-2: -On the Insert tab, in the Illustrations group, click SmartArt.

Concept note-3: -On the SmartArt Tools Design tab, in the Create Graphic group, click the Add Shape drop-down arrow. Select whether to add the shape before, after, above, or below the currently selected shape.

Concept note-4: -You can find SmartArt tools on two tabs: the Format tab and the Design tab: The Design tab helps you change your SmartArt graphicâ€™s layout, to change the style, or to change the colors used in your SmartArt graphic. The Format tab helps you change the shapes in your SmartArt graphic, their style, and their colors.
