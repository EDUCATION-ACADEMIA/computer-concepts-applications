Which method can be used to rearrange the order of slides in your presentation?

(A) Press SHIFT+UP ARROW to move the slide up one position.

(B) Right-click the slide and drag it to a new position within the slide thumbnail panel.

(C) Select Move Slide from the Format menu.

(D) ** Drag the slide to a new position within the slide thumbnail panel.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To select multiple slides: Press and hold Ctrl, and in the pane on the left, click each slide that you want to move. Release the Ctrl key, and then drag the selected slides as a group to the new location.

Concept note-2: -Changing slide order If the slides you want to move around are next to each other, you can “drag and drop” them in the Normal View.

Concept note-3: -On the Transitions tab, under Transition to This Slide, click the transition that you want-Fade, for example. To vary the transition, such as the direction it moves on the screen, click Effect Options, and then select the variation you want.

Concept note-4: -On the Window menu, click Arrange All to display both presentations. Click the slide you want to move, and drag it to the other presentation. If you want to copy the slide across then hold down Ctrl as you drag the slide. Note: To select more than one slide, press Ctrl while you click.

Concept note-5: -Changing the Slide Order The first way is using the Slide Sort command in the ribbon.
