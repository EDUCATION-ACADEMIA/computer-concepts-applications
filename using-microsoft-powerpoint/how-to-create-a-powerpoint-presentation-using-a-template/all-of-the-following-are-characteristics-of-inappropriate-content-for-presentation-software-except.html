HOW TO CREATE A POWERPOINT PRESENTATION USING A TEMPLATE

All of the following are characteristics of Inappropriate Content for Presentation Software EXCEPT:

(A) ** Educational material such as public information, health issues, or broadcasts

(B) Items presented from an original speech, historical declaration, or a memoir and should be preserved in the same manner.

(C) Trivial content or information that does not have a clear purpose

(D) Content is so dissected from its original form when translated into presentation that its meaning has been lost

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Inappropriate content includes information or images that upset your child, material that’s directed at adults, inaccurate information or information that might lead or tempt your child into unlawful or dangerous behaviour. This could be: Pornographic material. Content containing swearing.

Concept note-2: -Social media: risks uploading inappropriate content, like embarrassing or provocative photos or videos of themselves or others. sharing personal information with strangers – for example, images, date of birth, location or address. cyberbullying. being exposed to too much targeted advertising and marketing.

Concept note-3: -Children who see inappropriate content might feel: confusion or uncertainty. shame or guilt. shock or disgust.
