HOW TO CREATE A POWERPOINT PRESENTATION USING A TEMPLATE

[SOURCES]
Janet is representing her company at a job fair. They have a presentation running on a small TV screen, which Janet wants to repeat on its own throughout the day. Terry told her to just manually restart the slide show from the beginning each time it ends. What should Janet tell Terry?

(A) Modify the presentation’s setup to advance slides ‘on click’ works better.

(B) Adding a hyperlink to the presentation to the school’s webpage will restart it automatically.

(C) ** Janet can set the presentation to loop the slide show continuously.

(D) Terry’s right; she should manually restart the slide show from the beginning each time it ends.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What should Janet tell Terry? Janet can set the presentation to loop the slide show continuously.

Concept note-2: -Timer. The timer is located at the top-left corner of the current slide. It keeps track of how long Presenter view has been running.

Concept note-3: -What is the best method to use when you want to share a presentation but are not sure of whom to include in the target audience? Share as a link.

Concept note-4: -Use the slide master to make global design changes and create exactly the look and layouts you need. To work on the slide master, click the VIEW tab, Slide Master.

Concept note-5: -Any type of motion or movement that occurs in your presentation is called? An animation effect that occurs when a presentation moves from slide to slide in Slide Show view. Explore all similar answers.
