Active listening is applied in which of the following scenarios? KLO 2.3

(A) Jana published a manual which illustrates how to install Windows 10 with images

(B) ** Jana explained a step by step process in a class on how to run a c++ code while the trainees a working on their PCs

(C) Jana is delivering a local training and the trainees are focusing on her and take notes from time to time

(D) Jana supported a client on troubleshooting excel by using Zoom to share her desktop with him

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pay Attention Give the speaker your undivided attention, and acknowledge the message. Recognize that non-verbal communication also “speaks” loudly. Look at the speaker directly. Put aside distracting thoughts.

Concept note-2: -The listening process involves four stages: receiving, understanding, evaluating, and responding.
