USING THE INSERT AND DESIGN MENUS IN POWERPOINT

[SOURCES]
Images provided in a gallery with Microsoft are called?

(A) Intel

(B) Image Search

(C) ** Clip Art

(D) Microsoft Gallery

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Clip Art is the term given to pre-made images that are ready for use in your Microsoft Word documents. There are many Clip Art categories, such as Christmas, Special Occasions, People, Business, Decorative Elements, Animals, Cartoons and many more.

Concept note-2: -Clip art (also clipart, clip-art) is a type of graphic art. Pieces are pre-made images used to illustrate any medium. Today, clip art is used extensively and comes in many forms, both electronic and printed. However, most clip art today is created, distributed, and used in a digital form.

Concept note-3: -On the Insert tab of the toolbar ribbon, in the Images section, select Clip Art. and then you’ll see the Insert menu and the Clip Art icon.) The Clip Art task panel appears on the right side of the application window. In the Search for box, type keywords that describe the art you’re looking for.

Concept note-4: -Clip art is a collection of pictures or images that can be imported into a document or another program. The images may be either raster graphics or vector graphics. Clip art galleries many contain anywhere from a few images to hundreds of thousands of images.
