HOW TO CREATE A BLANK POWERPOINT PRESENTATION

[SOURCES]
These are the menu that you can find on PowerPoint, EXCEPT ____

(A) ** Blank Page

(B) Animation Pane

(C) Video

(D) Chart

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The menu toolbar across the top of the PowerPoint window includes all the commands accessible on the toolbars as well as additional commands.

Concept note-2: -It is made up of seven tabs; Home, Insert, Design, Animations, Slide Show, Review and View. Each tab is divided into groups of related commands that are displayed on the Ribbon. To view additional commands of a group click the arrow at the right bottom corner of the group.

Concept note-3: -From the presentation editing page, click Add Slides. Select Create a blank slide from the Add Slides options, as shown in the below figure.

Concept note-4: -The Insert Menu You can add a table to your slide, add a picture or create a photo album, add word art or even add video or audio to your slide.
