USING MICROSOFT POWERPOINT

HOW TO IMPORT TEXT FILES INTO YOUR POWERPOINT PRESENTATION

[SOURCES]
A(n) ____ is a series of images that represent the slides you intend to create.

(A) theme

(B) presentation

(C) ** storyboard

(D) Slide Show

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A storyboard is a graphic representation of how your video will unfold, shot by shot. It’s made up of a number of squares with illustrations or pictures representing each shot, with notes about what’s going on in the scene and what’s being said in the script during that shot.

Concept note-2: -What is a storyboard in PowerPoint? A storyboard in PowerPoint is a chart that shows a sequence of content. It typically contains a series of images, illustrations, drawings, screenshots or other visual graphics with captions for each.

Concept note-3: -Elements of a Storyboard Each shot of a storyboard captures several key elements: subject, background, camera shot, and the camera’s movement.
