HOW TO IMPORT TEXT FILES INTO YOUR POWERPOINT PRESENTATION

How can I print only the ODD number pages of a presentation?

(A) choosing Print “3 Slides”

(B) ** choosing Print > Pages > Custom, and typing 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 11 etc ____

(C) delete the odd number pages, Print, then UNDO with CTRL Z

(D) choosing Print > Pages > Custom and typing 1-13

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Print odd and even pages Click the File tab. Click Print. Under Settings, click Print All Pages. Near the bottom of the gallery, click Only Print Odd Pages.

Concept note-2: -Select the Settings for your PowerPoint printouts. The first drop-down menu lets you choose which slides you want to print. Here is where you could print just the odd pages first and then the even pages if you’re manually duplexing.

Concept note-3: -Open the print dialog by pressing Ctrl + P . In the General tab, choose Pages from the Range section. Type the numbers of the pages you want to print in the text box, separated by commas. Use a dash to denote a range of pages.

Concept note-4: -Then, click on the File tab at the top of the screen and select Print from the drop-down menu. In the Print dialog box that appears, click on the Setup button. Under Page Options, select the radio button next to Odd Pages Only or Even Pages Only. Then, click on the OK button at the bottom of the dialog box.
