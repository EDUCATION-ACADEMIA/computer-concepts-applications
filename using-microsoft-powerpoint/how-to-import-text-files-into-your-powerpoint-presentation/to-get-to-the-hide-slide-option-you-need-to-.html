HOW TO IMPORT TEXT FILES INTO YOUR POWERPOINT PRESENTATION

[SOURCES]
To get to the “Hide Slide” option you need to ____

(A) Click and drag the slide to the hide section

(B) ** Right Click on the slide

(C) Double click the slide

(D) Left Click on the slide

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Right-click the slide, and then select Hide Slide. The slide now shows a slash through the slide number to indicate that it’s hidden.

Concept note-2: -To do this, select the slides that you want to hide, right-click on them and choose Skip slide. You’ll see the icon of a crossed-out eye, which means these slides won’t be visible in the presentation. To make them visible again, just repeat the same process.

Concept note-3: -Display the slide you want to hide. Press Alt > S (Alt and then S) to access the Slide Show tab in the Ribbon. Press H to select Hide Slide.

Concept note-4: -Reading view The views in PowerPoint that you can use to edit, print, and deliver your presentation are as follows: Normal view. Slide Sorter view. Notes Page view.
