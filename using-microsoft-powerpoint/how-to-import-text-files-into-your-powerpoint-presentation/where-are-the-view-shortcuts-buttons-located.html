Where are the View Shortcuts buttons located?

(A) One the Office button

(B) On the View tab

(C) In the Clipboard group

(D) ** On the status bar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There are three view buttons on the right end of the status bar, to the left of the zoom control. These buttons provide different options to view the document.

Concept note-2: -1. In Microsoft Office programs, such as Excel, PowerPoint, and Word, the view buttons are a feature that lets you change how the presentation or document appears. The picture shows examples of the view buttons in Excel, PowerPoint, and Word in the bottom-right corner of the program.

Concept note-3: -The view button on status bar is used to see many programs. It helps in increasing Sound or see the printing status etc.

Concept note-4: -Answer. There are three Page View buttons on the Excel Status Bar.
