FORMATTING YOUR POWERPOINT PRESENTATION USING SLIDE MASTERS AND LAYOUTS

[SOURCES]
Which of the following statements about hiding slides is true?

(A) Hidden slides are removed from the presentation file and cannot be retrieved.

(B) Hidden slides are removed from the Slide Navigation pane but remain in the presentation file.

(C) ** Hidden slides can be unhidden by clicking the Hide Slide option.

(D) Hidden files can be retrieved by clicking Manage Presentation in the Backstage view.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -While editing slides in Normal view, in the navigation pane on the left, you can hide or unhide a slide, as described below. In Slide Sorter view, you can use the same method to hide or unhide a slide. And while youâ€™re presenting to an audience, you can unhide a slide, as described below.

Concept note-2: -If necessary, click the View tab in the Ribbon and select Normal in the Presentation Views group. Display the slide you want to unhide. Press Alt > S (Alt and then S) to access the Slide Show tab in the Ribbon. Press H to select Hide Slide (which will unhide a hidden slide).

Concept note-3: -We right-click over the slide in question at the navigation bar and click on the option Hide Slide.

Concept note-4: -The Slide Sorter View is convenient if you are hiding multiple slides at a time. To go to a hidden slide during your presentation, right-click anywhere on the current slide, choose the By Title option, and then pick your hidden slide. The hidden slide will have parentheses around the slide number.
