FORMATTING YOUR POWERPOINT PRESENTATION USING SLIDE MASTERS AND LAYOUTS

[SOURCES]
Define template:

(A) ** A predesigned presentation.

(B) A scheme of complementing colors.

(C) A small picture of a slide.

(D) Additional information associated with a slide.

(E) A printed copy of a presentation.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pre-designed Templates allows you to offer your customers editable designs instead of having them starting from scratch to personalize your products. You can create designs with editable texts and images and freely define a set of customization rules and restrictions for each design or element of the design.

Concept note-2: -A template is a predesigned presentation you can use to create a new slide show quickly.

Concept note-3: -A PowerPoint template is a pattern or blueprint of a slide or group of slides that you save as a . potx file. Templates can contain layouts, colors, fonts, effects, background styles, and even content. You can create your own custom templates and store them, reuse them, and share them with others.

Concept note-4: -1-Templates are not the pre-defined styles or designs which are present in the system. 2-placeholders cannot hold objects such as text, clips, art etc. See what the community says and unlock a badge.
