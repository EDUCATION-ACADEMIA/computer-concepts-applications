USING THE DRAWING TOOLS AND FORMAT FEATURES IN POWERPOINT

[SOURCES]
What method is used to crop an image to a specific shape, such as a heart?

(A) Picture Layout

(B) Crop to Fit

(C) Picture Styles

(D) ** Crop to Shape

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -If you want to change the outline of a picture and make it a shape (like a circle or a star), use the cropping tools on the PICTURE TOOLS FORMAT tab. Select the picture (or pictures) that you want to crop. On the PICTURE TOOLS FORMAT tab, click Crop > Crop to Shape, and then pick the shape you want.

Concept note-2: -Use the Cookie Cutter tool The Cookie Cutter tool crops a photo into a shape that you choose. Select a shape and drag it on your photo to crop the photo in that shape. You can also move and resize the bounding box to get the desired area you want to crop.

Concept note-3: -To “crop” an image is to remove or adjust the outside edges of an image (typically a photo) to improve framing or composition, draw a viewer’s eye to the image subject, or change the size or aspect ratio. In other words, image cropping is the act of improving a photo or image by removing the unnecessary parts.
