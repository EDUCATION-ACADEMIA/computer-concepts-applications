Which of the two pieces of hardware play a crucial role in the quality of images and videos in a computer?

(A) Hard Disk Drive

(B) ** The screen (monitor)

(C) RAM

(D) ** GPU

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So, whereas the CPU is the most important component for most video editing software, having a powerful graphics card is critical to editing successfully with this software. It can take nearly linear advantage of GPU power, including multiple GPUs in SLI or CrossFire.

Concept note-2: -A computer monitor is an output device that displays information in pictorial or textual form. A discrete monitor comprises a visual display, support electronics, power supply, housing, electrical connectors, and external user controls.

Concept note-3: -GPU. Your Graphics Processing Unit (GPU) is responsible for rendering images, animations and video to be displayed on your screen.

Concept note-4: -While the terms GPU and graphics card (or video card) are often used interchangeably, there is a subtle distinction between these terms. Much like a motherboard contains a CPU, a graphics card refers to an add-in board that incorporates the GPU.
