We’re late. The film ____ by the time we get to the cinema.

(A) will already start

(B) will be already started

(C) ** will already have started

(D) will be starting

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The film WILL HAVE STARTED by the time we reach the cinema. This is the correct expression. We’re already late, the film will have started by the time we get to the cinema.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is B)had already started.

Concept note-3: -One way to show the passage of time is through camera movement and editing. The actors might be in one place, but the camera could move over to another location where the actor was at a different time.

Concept note-4: -Its go to the cinema if you do not know the name of the cinema, if using go the cinema, you would use it in a phrase like let’s go to west acres cinema. Exactly the same with most phrases in this context. Let’s go to the shopping mall or let’s go to west acres shopping.

Concept note-5: -’At the cinema’ refers to the function of the cinema. People watch films when they’re at the cinema. ‘In the cinema’ refers to being inside the building itself. For example, you might hear this: ‘Following the attack, police interviewed witnesses who were in the bank and in the cinema.
