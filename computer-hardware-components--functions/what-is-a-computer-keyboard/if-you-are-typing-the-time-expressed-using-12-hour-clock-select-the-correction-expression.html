If you are typing the time expressed using 12 hour clock, select the correction expression?

(A) ** 9.45 a.m.

(B) 0945 hours

(C) 9..45 a.m

(D) 9;45 a.m

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -20:55 time in the 12-hour clock time convention is 08:55 pm. Regular time, normal time and standard time are synonym with the twelve hour clock. 20:55 is the 24-hour clock equivalent of 08:55 pm. Military time means the 24-hour clock time convention without a colon between the hours and minutes: 2055.

Concept note-2: -There are two ways of telling the time: The 12-hour clock runs from 1am to 12 noon and then from 1pm to 12 midnight. The 24-hour clock uses the numbers 00:00 to 23:59 (midnight is 00:00).

Concept note-3: -Hence, 9:20 PM in 24 hour format will be 12:00 + 9:20 = 21:20.

Concept note-4: -09:10 on a 24-hour clock is is 9:10 AM on a 12-hour clock.
