One main advantage of Portable hard disk drive compared to internal hard disk:

(A) More stronger and durable compared to internal HDD

(B) ** easy to move and transfer data/files from one desktop system to another

(C) is Cheaper than internal HDD

(D) Where Operating system is stored and is essential part of desktop computers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -External drives are ideal for raw storage space and the transportation of files. Internal drives are suitable for frequent file access and speed, especially with gaming. External drives can also be easier to repair and have a higher storage cap, while internal drives can hold up better and last longer.

Concept note-2: -The best advantage of owning an external drive is that they are extremely portable â€“ they can be connected to numerous computers and allow you to carry a large amount of storage with you regardless of your location.

Concept note-3: -An internal hard drive is built within the computer. An external hard drive is not. You need to connect it to the computer using a cable to have access to it. The main difference between an internal hard drive and an external hard drive is location.
