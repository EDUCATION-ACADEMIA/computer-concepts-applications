COMPUTER OUTPUT DEVICES

[SOURCES]
CD

(A) Input

(B) Output

(C) ** Storage

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A compact disc is a portable storage medium that can record, store and play back audio, video and other data in digital form.

Concept note-2: -A CD holds about 700 MB or 0.7 GB. A one TB drive holds 1, 000 GB. That amounts to 1, 428 CDs if they were 100% full.

Concept note-3: -Store in a cool, dry, dark environment in which the air is clean. Remove dirt, foreign material, fingerprints, smudges, and liquids by wiping with a clean cotton fabric in a straight line from the center of the disc toward the outer edge.

Concept note-4: -compact disc (CD), a molded plastic disc containing digital data that is scanned by a laser beam for the reproduction of recorded sound and other information.
