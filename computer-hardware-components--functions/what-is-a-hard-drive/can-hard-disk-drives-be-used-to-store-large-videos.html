Can hard disk drives be used to store large videos?

(A) ** Yes

(B) No

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Yes, a 500GB external hard drive should be enough for most users. It can hold around 100, 000 songs or up to 500 hours of HD video.

Concept note-2: -External HDD: large storage capacity Then an external hard drive, or external HDD (Hard Drive Disk), is the best choice for you. With an HDD you have a lot of storage space for a relatively low price. You can store 40 movies in 4K quality and more than 80, 000 high-resolution photos on a 4TB hard drive.

Concept note-3: -hard disk, also called hard disk drive or hard drive, magnetic storage medium for a computer. Hard disks are flat circular plates made of aluminum or glass and coated with a magnetic material. Hard disks for personal computers can store terabytes (trillions of bytes) of information.

Concept note-4: -A hard drive can store thousands of image files and hours of video footage. As well as being one of the best ways to store photos, they are also great for archiving invoices and other documents.

Concept note-5: -As of 2020 SSDs started to compete with HDDs. As of May 2022, the largest hard drive is 26 TB (while SSDs can be much bigger at 100 TB, mainstream consumer SSDs cap at 8 TB). Smaller, 2.5-inch drives, are available at up to 2TB for laptops, and 5TB as external drives.
